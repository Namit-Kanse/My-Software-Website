Custom Desktop Application

Paytha Dashboard: A Complete Digital Transformation

We automated manual bookkeeping, attendance, and billing into one unified application.

The Challenge

Time-consuming manual entry using register books, attendance cards, and handwritten bills.

The Solution

A unified desktop app with biometric integration, auto-salary calculation, and inventory management.

The Result

A massive 20+ hours of administrative work saved per week, plus improved accuracy and insights.

The Challenge

The client was managing their entire business using traditional, manual methods. All customer orders were tracked in register books, employee attendance was marked on paper cards, and all salaries were calculated by hand. Billing was done with handwritten books, which was time-consuming and prone to errors. They also had no way to track daily expenses or manage their inventory digitally.

The Solution

We developed the "Paytha Dashboard," a custom desktop application to centralize all operations. We integrated biometric attendance, which automatically logs employee hours. The app uses these hours to auto-calculate salaries based on per-hour rates. The new billing feature handles invoicing, delivery challans, and quotations, with direct Tally integration. We also built the requested features for daily expense tracking and a full inventory management system to track all purchased materials.

Key Features

One-Click Attendance Import

Seamlessly imports data from biometric devices, eliminating all manual attendance tracking and hour calculation.

Secure User Logins

Role-based access control ensures that employees and managers can only see the information relevant to them.

PDF Bill Generation

Instantly create, print, and email professional PDF bills, delivery challans, and quotations, saving time and reducing errors.
